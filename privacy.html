Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

This privacy policy covers the Org Chart Power BI custom visual published by Lotus Lotus on Microsoft AppSource.

Data collection

The visual does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. It runs entirely within the Microsoft Power BI environment and makes no network calls to external services. All data rendered by the visual is supplied by your Power BI report and remains within your Power BI environment at all times.

Cookies and tracking

The visual does not use cookies, analytics, telemetry, or any other form of tracking.

Third parties

No data handled by the visual is shared with, sold to, or made accessible to any third party. Your use of Power BI itself is governed by Microsoft's own privacy statement and your organisation's agreements with Microsoft.

This website

This website is a static site that sets no cookies and runs no analytics. It is hosted on GitHub Pages; GitHub may log standard technical information such as IP addresses for security purposes, as described in GitHub's privacy statement.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of the visual changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated before that version is published, and the date above will be revised.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to alex.lines@lotuslotus.co.uk.
